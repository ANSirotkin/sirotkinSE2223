Procedures


  1. Weigh and record mass of C. comosum before experiment

  2. Add plant to pot and add soil

  3. Measure correct amount of Trichoderma and add to plant (2g, 4g, or 6g)

  4. Repeat setup for all 48 plants (4 conditions of 12 trials each)

  5. Set up plants in trays in an array of 4 conditions by 12 trials

  1. Carry out experiment for 6 weeks

  2. Water plants with 1/16 quart of water every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday

  3. If any components fall out of pots, replace them with new ones and dispose of old ones

  1. Empty pots and obtain C. comosum specimens

  2. Clean C. comosum specimens by rinsing in tap water

  3. Dispose of the remaining contents of the pot

  4. Weigh and record mass of C. comosum after experiment
